Map Overview & Objectives

Outer Turrets

Protect the outer edges of each lane. Destroying them opens the map for aggression.

Jungle Buffs

Blue Buff: Mana regen & cooldown. Red Buff: Slow effect on attacks.

Dark Slayer

Major objective that grants team-wide buffs. Appears later in the game.

River Vision

Crucial for controlling rotations and ambushes. Place vision for better control.

Mini Camps

Smaller neutral camps for gold/XP. Clear regularly to level up efficiently.
